Stickies

Stickies is a simple yet powerful note-taking application for ryOS, allowing users to quickly jot down reminders and organize thoughts using colorful, persistent sticky notes right on their desktop. It provides an intuitive way to keep important information readily accessible within your digital workspace.

Overview

The Stickies app provides a convenient and familiar way to keep important information at your fingertips within the ryOS environment. It mimics the experience of physical sticky notes, but with the added benefits of digital persistence and easy organization. Users can create multiple notes, customize their colors for visual categorization, and rely on automatic saving to ensure their reminders are always there when they return. This app is ideal for quick memos, task lists, temporary data storage, or anything that needs to be readily accessible without the need for a full document editor.

Features

  • Effortless Note Creation: Quickly generate new sticky notes to capture thoughts the moment they arise, either via the menu or a simple keyboard shortcut.
  • Intuitive Color Organization: Assign different colors to notes to visually categorize information, making it easier to find and prioritize specific reminders.
  • Simple Note Management: Easily remove individual notes once they are no longer needed, helping to keep your workspace tidy.
  • Automatic Persistence: All notes are automatically saved and restored across ryOS sessions, ensuring your important reminders are never lost.
  • Interactive Interface: Notes can be freely moved and resized within the application window, adapting to your preferred layout.

User Guide

Getting Started

To launch Stickies, locate its icon in the ryOS Applications menu and click it. A new, blank sticky note will automatically appear, ready for your input. You can type directly into the note to begin.

Key Actions

  • Creating a New Note: To add another sticky note, click File in the menu bar, then select New Note. Alternatively, use the keyboard shortcut ⌘N.
  • Changing Note Color: Select a note by clicking on it. Then, navigate to the Note menu in the menu bar and choose a color from the available options to visually distinguish your notes.
  • Deleting a Note: To remove an individual sticky note, click the small "x" (close) button located in the top-right corner of the note itself. You can also select the note and use the keyboard shortcut ⌘W.
  • Clearing All Notes: To remove all sticky notes from your workspace, go to File > Clear All Notes.

Tips & Shortcuts

  • Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • ⌘N: Create a new sticky note.
    • ⌘W: Delete the currently selected sticky note.
  • Persistence: Remember that all your sticky notes are automatically saved. You don't need to manually save your work before closing the app or shutting down ryOS.
  • Organization: Utilize the color-coding feature to group similar tasks, prioritize urgent items, or simply make your notes more visually appealing and easier to navigate.

Technical Details

Window Configuration

  • Default size: 500×400px
  • Minimum size: 300×250px

Component Architecture

The app consists of 3 component file(s):
  • src/apps/stickies/components/StickiesMenuBar.tsx: Manages the application's top menu bar, handling actions like creating new notes, clearing all notes, and showing dialogs.
  • src/apps/stickies/components/StickyNote.tsx: Represents an individual sticky note, handling its content, color, position, and close button.
  • src/apps/stickies/components/StickiesAppComponent.tsx: The main application component that orchestrates the display of the menu bar and all individual sticky notes, coordinating their interactions.

Hooks & Utilities

Custom Hooks:
  • src/apps/stickies/hooks/useStickiesLogic.ts: This hook encapsulates the core business logic for the Stickies app, managing the collection of notes, their creation, deletion, color changes, and selection, as well as dialog states and persistence.

State Management

The Stickies app primarily manages its state using local React state, orchestrated by the useStickiesLogic custom hook. This includes the array of individual notes, the currently selectedNoteId, and the visibility of various dialogs (Help, About). Note data is persisted automatically between ryOS sessions, leveraging local browser storage mechanisms managed within the useStickiesLogic hook.

Related Apps

  • Text Editor: For more extensive note-taking or document creation that requires richer formatting, spell-checking, and file management capabilities.
  • Calendar: To integrate reminders and tasks from Stickies into a scheduled timeline, providing a broader view of your commitments.
