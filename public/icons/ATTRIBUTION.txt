Icon packs under `default/vendor/` are generated by
`bun run vendor:icons` (see `scripts/vendor-themed-icons.ts`).

Exports the **full** upstream sets (all PNGs from @react95/icons `png/`, and
all MacOS9 `png 64px` indices 1–92 that exist on the remote — 63 is absent).

Sources:
1) @react95/icons (npm), MIT — https://github.com/React95/React95
   (React95 LICENSE notes Microsoft marks are not covered by MIT.)

2) bearz314/MacOS9-icons, MIT — https://github.com/bearz314/MacOS9-icons

Vendor output does not replace top-level `default/`, `macosx/`, `win98/`, or
`xp/` icons. Re-run `vendor:icons` to refresh scaled copies under `vendor/`.
